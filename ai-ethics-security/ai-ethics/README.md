# AI Ethics Research

This directory contains research on ethical considerations in artificial intelligence development, deployment, and governance.

## Research Topics

### 1. Responsible AI Frameworks
- OECD AI Principles
- EU AI Act requirements
- IEEE Ethically Aligned Design
- Partnership on AI guidelines
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework

### 2. Bias & Fairness
- Types of AI bias (selection, confirmation, automation)
- Bias detection methodologies
- Fairness metrics and definitions
- Mitigation strategies and techniques
- Ongoing monitoring approaches

### 3. Transparency & Explainability
- Explainable AI (XAI) techniques
- Model interpretability methods
- Documentation requirements (Model Cards, Datasheets)
- User-facing explanations
- Regulatory disclosure requirements

### 4. Human Oversight & Control
- Human-in-the-loop systems
- Human-on-the-loop monitoring
- Override mechanisms and kill switches
- Accountability frameworks
- Decision authority boundaries

### 5. Societal Impact
- Economic displacement considerations
- Environmental impact of AI training
- Dual-use technology concerns
- Digital divide implications
- Long-term societal effects

### 6. AI Governance
- Organizational AI ethics boards
- Policy development frameworks
- Incident response procedures
- Whistleblower protections
- Third-party auditing

## Key Regulations & Standards

| Regulation/Standard | Jurisdiction | Focus |
|---------------------|--------------|-------|
| EU AI Act | European Union | Risk-based AI regulation |
| NIST AI RMF | United States | Risk management framework |
| C-27 (AIDA) | Canada | AI and data governance |
| AI Governance Framework | Singapore | Ethics and governance |
| ISO/IEC 42001 | International | AI management systems |
