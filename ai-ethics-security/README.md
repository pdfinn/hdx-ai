# AI Ethics, Data, Security & Product Development Research

This directory contains comprehensive research on AI ethics, data governance, security practices, and product development methodologies for responsible AI systems.

## Research Areas

### [AI Ethics](./ai-ethics/)
Research on ethical considerations in AI development, deployment, and governance including:
- Responsible AI frameworks and principles
- Bias detection and mitigation strategies
- Transparency and explainability requirements
- Human oversight and control mechanisms
- Societal impact assessments

### [Data Governance](./data-governance/)
Research on data management, privacy, and compliance including:
- Data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Data quality and integrity frameworks
- Consent management and user rights
- Data lifecycle management
- Cross-border data transfer considerations

### [Security](./security/)
Research on AI security and cybersecurity practices including:
- AI/ML security vulnerabilities and mitigations
- Adversarial attack prevention
- Model security and integrity
- Infrastructure security for AI systems
- Compliance frameworks (SOC2, ISO 27001, FedRAMP)

### [Product Development](./product-development/)
Research on responsible AI product development including:
- AI product lifecycle management
- Risk assessment methodologies
- Testing and validation frameworks
- Deployment and monitoring best practices
- User experience considerations for AI products

### [Executive Education](./executive-education/)
Curriculum and research materials for executive MBA programs:
- Introduction to GenAI: Data and Project Management (1 hour)
- Introduction to GenAI: Ethics, Security, and Implementation (2 hours)
- Strategic frameworks for executive decision-making
- Case studies and discussion questions

## Research Methodology

Each research area follows a consistent methodology:
1. **Literature Review** - Academic and industry sources
2. **Regulatory Analysis** - Current and emerging regulations
3. **Best Practices** - Industry standards and frameworks
4. **Case Studies** - Real-world implementations
5. **Recommendations** - Actionable guidance
